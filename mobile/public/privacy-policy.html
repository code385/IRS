IRS Timesheet – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the IRS Timesheet mobile application and related services.

Effective date: 3 March 2026
Owner: Infrastructure Renewal Services Pty Ltd

1. Information We Collect

When you use IRS Timesheet, we may collect:

  • Account information – name, email address, and basic profile details.
  • Timesheet data – work dates, hours, projects, tasks, comments and attachments.
  • Device information – device model, OS version, app version for diagnostics.
  • Usage data – how you interact with the app to improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the IRS Timesheet app and its features.
  • Store and display timesheets, drafts, approvals, and reports.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and protect the system.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate updates.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Storage and Security

IRS Timesheet uses Firebase by Google to store data securely. We use authentication, access controls, and encrypted connections (HTTPS). We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third‑Party Services

We use:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – authentication, database, and hosting.
  • Expo – build and update services for the mobile app.

We do not sell or rent your personal information for marketing.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
